Debate: Colonoscopy vs. FIT
March is Colorectal Cancer Awareness Month, and this issue of AGA
Perspectives starts off with an insightful point-counterpoint debate of
the merits of FIT testing versus colonoscopy for colon cancer prevention.
Compelling arguments for both sides of this debate are presented by Douglas Rex, MD, AGAF, (colonoscopy) and Douglas Robertson, MD, MPH (FIT).
