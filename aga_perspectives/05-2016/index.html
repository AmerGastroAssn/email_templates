Elastography: Covering the Spectrum?
Drs. Stuart Gordon and Keyur Patel review the pros and cons of elastography technology in this issue's feature story. Dr. Gordon advocates for the growing adoption of the technology, while Dr. Patel presents an overview of potential drawbacks.
Featured Articles
Chronic Cough: Why it Could Be GERD and Why it May Not Be
Drs. David Francis and Michael Vaezi discuss cough evaluation in patients who may have GERD.
Promises and Limitations: Translating Microbiome Research into Practice
Although targeting the microbiome is not yet a cure all, there are a few areas where microbiome research will be key in practice, write Drs. Ece Mutlu and Purna Kashyap.
