Featured Debate
GERD Testing: Conduct On or Off Therapy
Philip O. Katz, MD, FACG, AGAF, makes the case that patients will be well served with a well-performed combined transnasal impedance/pH study on therapy as long as the decision to perform reflux monitoring is individualized. While Ronnie Fass, MD, argues that performing the pH tests off therapy allows us to determine the underlying disorder responsible for a patient’s GERD-like symptoms.
