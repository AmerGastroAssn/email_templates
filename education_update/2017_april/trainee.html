AGA Education Update — GI Fellow Edition

As a GI professional with a limited amount of time for specialized learning, you choose the continuing medical education programs that best fit your busy schedule each year. The 2017 AGA Postgraduate Course is your chance to absorb a mix of gastroenterology updates in a short span of time. Attend six general sessions, each covering a different topic, plus breakout sessions for more enhanced learning — all in 1.5-days.

The course will be held May 6 and 7 during Digestive Disease Week® (May 6–9). It is pending up to 12 AMA PRA Category 1 Credits™ and 12 American Board of Internal Medicine maintenance of certification points.

It's not too late to register.
Review the schedule, pricing and course faculty videos online. Then register to secure your spot before it’s too late. AGA trainee and student members can register for just $165.
