AGA Education Update

Admit one to the first and foremost GI meeting in the world. Get inspired for the next year by attending AGA’s section-led sessions at Digestive Disease Week® (DDW) 2017, May 6-9, in Chicago, IL. For additional program highlights and details regarding AGA’s 13 special interest sections, read the AGA Highlights at DDW 2017 booklet. Read now.

Early bird pricing for both DDW and the AGA Postgraduate Couse ends on March 22. Be sure to register on or before this date to receive deep discounts on the cost to attend. If you are already a confirmed attendee, you can visit the Registration Resource Center to add ticketed sessions to your registration.
