The Full Scope of GI Advances

The 2017 AGA Postgraduate Course Resources allow you to virtually experience the live course from home and provide a rapid way for you to learn the latest treatment advances.

Along with the new Hot Topics general session, you will learn about current approaches to treating five diseases states — disorders of the gut and upper GI tract, functional GI disorders, liver and pancreatic biliary disease, IBD and hepatitis. Gauge your learning and apply your knowledge while improving patient care by immediately implementing key actions items in your practice.
