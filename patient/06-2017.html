IBS: A Guide to Irritable Bowel Syndrome helps provide your patients who may be suffering from IBS with a visually appealing and easy-to-read printed brochure to help them better understand the condition and treatment options.

The printed brochure offers facts about the condition and how it’s identified, as well as encouragement to patients for them to speak up early, completely and often about their symptoms. The whole brochure is written with patients in mind, in language they can relate to. Best of all, thanks to a grant from Ironwood Pharmaceuticals, these printed brochures, complete with symptom tracking charts, are free to AGA members. You just pay for shipping.

The brochure includes information on:

  • What IBS Is.
  • Possible Causes.
  • Symptoms.
  • Bowel Movements: What is "Normal."
  • Getting Tested.
  • Preparing for the Doctor's Visit.
  • Newly Diagnosed with IBS.
  • Treatment.
  • Questions for Your Doctor.
