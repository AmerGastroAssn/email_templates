TIP OF THE MONTH • april 2016  

Throughout your first year as an AGA member, look forward to monthly tips with essential information for making the most of your membership. Below is the tip for April 2016.

As an AGA member, you can take advantage of special pricing on clinical products and services offered by our affinity partners. These tools and resources can help you manage your practice, treat your patients and thrive in your career.

  • Learn more about medical conditions and treatment options with UpToDate®.
  • Save time and make more informed clinical decisions with Epocrates®.
  • Find or fill a position with GICareerSearch.com.
  • Access valuable patient education information from Shire UCentral.
  • Provide your patients with tools from Takeda, which allow them to discreetly track symptoms and behaviors for IBS and constipation.

Questions about your membership? Contact AGA Member Relations at 301-941-2651 or member@gastro.org.
