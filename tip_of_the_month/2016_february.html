TIP OF THE MONTH • FEBRUARY 2016

Throughout your first year as an AGA member, you can look forward to monthly tips with essential information for making the most of your membership. Below is the tip for February 2016.

We realize it’s hard to make time to stay up-to-date on the latest GI news and developments with your busy schedule. As an AGA member, keeping informed is easy and convenient by reading AGA publications online anytime and anywhere.

You can view all resources at www.gastro.org/publications or on the publications section of the website.

If you are in a membership category that receives journal subscriptions, you don't need to wait to receive your printed issue in the mail. You can view Gastroenterology, Clinical Gastroenterology and Hepatology, and Cellular and Molecular Gastroenterology and Hepatology online.*

To access the latest and largest body of knowledge in the GI community:

  1. Visit www.gastro.org/publications.
  2. Select the “Members” button below the journal name and log in with the same email address and personal password you use for AGA’s website.

*You can also read the journals on your mobile devices.
