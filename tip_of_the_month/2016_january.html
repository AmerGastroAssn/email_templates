TIP OF THE MONTH • JANUARY 2016

Throughout your first year as an AGA member, you can look forward to monthly tips with essential information for making the most of your membership. Below is the tip for January 2016.

As an AGA member you have full access to all the resources on gastro.org. Log in to access valuable members-only information, discounted products and online publications, and to check out what’s new throughout the year.

With My AGA, you can easily:

  • Update your profile information.
  • Change your password and customize your membership experience.
  • Manage your email subscriptions, section affiliations and committee information.
  • Track the CME credits you have earned through AGA.

Get started today by visiting www.gastro.org and entering your username and password in the “Member Login” box at the top right. If you have not changed your login information, your username is the email address indicated on your membership application and the password is your member ID.
