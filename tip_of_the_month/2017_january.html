TIP OF THE MONTH • January
Throughout your first year as an AGA member, look forward to monthly tips with essential information for making the most of your membership. Below is the tip for January 2017.

The fastest and easiest way to access all your member benefits is by logging into www.gastro.org. Take advantage of your benefits with online access to members-only resources and to receive your member discounts on products and publications. Be sure to update your profile at My-AGA and share your interests to ensure you receive only the most relevant news and information from AGA.

Visit My-AGA today to:

  • Update your contact information and ensure you’re receiving the most up-to-date information.
  • Customize your member experience by updating your communications preferences.
  • Manage your section affiliations, committee information, AGA events and more.
  • Keep track of the CME credits you have earned through AGA.

Visit www.gastro.org today and login to update your member profile. If you have any questions about your membership, please contact Member Relations at member@gastro.org or call 301-941-2651.
