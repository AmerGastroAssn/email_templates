TIP OF THE MONTH • JULY 2017
Throughout your first year as an AGA member, look forward to monthly tips with essential information for making the most of your membership. Below is the tip for July 2017.

AGA works for you on Capitol Hill as the voice of GI. AGA's top issues are:

  • Fair reimbursement
  • Increased NIH funding
  • Colonoscopy cost sharing
  • Medical liability reform
  • In-office ancillary services
  • Payment stability for Ambulatory Surgery Centers
  • Patient access

To advance this mission, AGA's government affairs department:

  • Works with government and regulatory agencies to protect the needs of GIs.
  • Develops and implements a rigorous public policy agenda.
  • Gives GIs a greater presence on Capitol Hill with AGA PAC, the only political action committee supported by a national gastroenterological society.
  • Provides opportunities for you to contact your legislators.

Visit www.gastro.org/advocacy to learn more.
