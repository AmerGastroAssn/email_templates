TIP OF THE MONTH • MARCH
Throughout your first year as an AGA member, look forward to monthly tips with essential information for making the most of your membership. Below is the tip for March 2017.

Planning to attend Digestive Disease Week® (DDW) 2017 in Chicago, IL., May 6–9? You should know, members receive a discount to attend, even after the early bird deadline. DDW is the world’s largest gathering of professionals in the fields of gastroenterology, hepatology, endoscopy and gastrointestinal surgery. This is your opportunity to join nearly 16,000 of your colleagues for the first and foremost GI event in the world. As an added bonus, DDW on demand is also included with your registration.

Plan ahead with DDW’s pre-meeting resources to maximize your time and experience on site. We encourage you to connect with DDW on social media to get live and up-to-date information both before and during the meeting, as well as see what attractions await you in Chicago.
