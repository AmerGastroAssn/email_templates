TIP OF THE MONTH • May 
Throughout your first year as an AGA member, look forward to monthly tips with essential information for making the most of your membership. Below is the tip for May 2017.

Connect with AGA online and on social media to stay up-to-date with the latest news, trends and topics in GI. You can also use AGA’s social networks to stay in touch and connect with other AGA members. See below for more information about AGA social media sites.

  JOIN AGA COMMUNITY

Network with colleagues in AGA Community, our lively member-only discussion forum, resource library and directory. Connect with others who share the same professional interests on diverse topics within the ever-changing GI field.

  LIKE AGA ON FACEBOOK

Like AGA on Facebook and receive regular updates relevant to AGA members and the GI community. Find and post photos from events and participate in informative discussions around hot topics in GI care, research and policy. For the latest AGA journal news, make sure to also follow Gastroenterology, Clinical Gastroenterology and Hepatology and CMGH.

  FOLLOW AGA ON TWITTER

Follow AGA on Twitter and join the more than 16,800 followers who use Twitter to stay up-to-date on the latest GI industry news and advances in the field. AGA consistently posts journal research, event updates, policy articles and more.

  SUBSCRIBE TO AGA CONTENT ON YOUTUBE

Subscribe to AGA’s YouTube channel and view Gastro and CGH video abstracts from GI experts, receive updates from AGA leaders, learn about the AGA Research Foundation, find patient videos you can play in your practice, and much more.

FIND COLLEAGUES IN THE AGA MEMBER DIRECTORY

Access the online AGA Member Directory to connect directly with your colleagues and peers in the field.

P.S. Please update your AGA member profile and communications preferences so AGA can customize the content to fit your needs and interests.
