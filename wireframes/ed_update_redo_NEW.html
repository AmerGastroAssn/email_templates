Digestive Digest Week ® (DDW) 2016
Abstract Submission for DDW Ends Dec. 1

Present your research at the premier meeting for GI, hepatology, endoscopy and GI surgery professionals. Don't forget, abstract submission closes at 9 p.m. ET on Dec. 1. Please note the deadline is the Tuesday after the U.S. Thanksgiving holiday. Visit the DDW website to submit and view a variety of resources that will help you navigate the submission process.

DDW will take place May 21–24, 2016, in San Diego, CA. Members-only registration opens Jan. 6, 2016.

DDW is co-sponsored by AGA, AASLD, ASGE and SSAT.

What is Your Gut Microbiota and What Does it Mean?

In this special offer through a collaboration between the AGA Center for Gut Microbiome Research and Education and the American Gut Project, we will provide you with a customized analysis and interpretation of your gut microbiota (view an example). Interesting trends in the volunteers’ de-identified results will be presented at a special DDW 2016 session, Active Learning Session on the Gut Microbiome: Fundamental Principles in Theory and Practice.

The call for volunteers is open until Dec. 7, 2015. You must be an AGA member who resides in the U.S. A home sampling kit will be sent to you for a small fee of $75. Learn more.
