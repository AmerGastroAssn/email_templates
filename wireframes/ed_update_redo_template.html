Free Access to HCV Accredited Toolkit to Improve Patient Care
This toolkit contains four brief CME videos that individually address important aspects of HCV patient care, and two CME monographs on treatment efficacy, adherence, and documentation. These accredited activities were planned in conjunction with the AGA Institute and PRIME®, through independent educational grants from Gilead Sciences, Inc. and AbbVie, Inc. The toolkit also includes a patient engagement package and enrollment forms for your patients. You can view these free HCV programs at www.cmetoolkit.com.
